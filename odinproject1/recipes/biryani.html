Biryani


Description

Biryani is a flavorful layered rice dish with marinated meat, fragrant spices, and herbs, cooked to perfection.

Ingredients

    • Basmati rice – 2 cups
      Chicken or mutton – 500 grams, marinated
      Yogurt – 1/2 cup
      Onion – 2 large, thinly sliced
      Tomatoes – 2 medium, chopped
      Ginger-garlic paste – 2 tbsp
      Biryani masala – 2 tbsp
      Saffron – a few strands soaked in warm milk
      Fresh mint and coriander leaves – chopped
      Oil or ghee – 3 tbsp
      Salt to taste

    • Steps :

    • Cook the basmati rice partially and drain the water.
      Fry onions until golden and set aside some for garnish.
      Cook marinated meat with tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, and yogurt till tender.
      Layer the cooked meat and rice in a heavy-bottom pot, sprinkle saffron milk, mint, coriander, and fried onions.
      Seal and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes for the flavors to blend.
      Serve hot with raita or salad.
