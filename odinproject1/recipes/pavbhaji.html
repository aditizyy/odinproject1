Pav Bhaji


Description

Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food consisting of a spicy mashed vegetable curry served with butter-toasted bread rolls called pav.

Ingredients

    • Potatoes – 3 medium, boiled and mashed
      Green peas – 1/2 cup, boiled
      Cauliflower – 1 cup, finely chopped and boiled
      Tomatoes – 3 medium, finely chopped
      Onion – 1 large, finely chopped
      Green chili – 1, finely chopped
      Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tbsp
      Pav bhaji masala – 2 tbsp
      Butter – 3 tbsp
      Salt to taste
      Fresh coriander leaves – chopped, for garnish
      Lemon wedges and pav (bread rolls) to serve

    • Steps :

    • Sauté onions, green chili, and ginger-garlic paste in butter until golden brown.
      Add tomatoes and cook till soft and mushy.
      Add boiled vegetables, pav bhaji masala, and salt and mash everything together.
      Simmer the mixture for 10 minutes to blend flavors.
      Serve hot garnished with coriander leaves, lemon wedges, and butter-toasted pav.
